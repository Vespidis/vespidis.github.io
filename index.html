My Project

Summary

The intention of the project (GlobeTouch) is to provider customers of the Cruising (and travel) industry, an interactive booking resource that provides a more dynamic experience. This user interface will allow guests to have a more kinaesthetic experience when booking their holidays, by allowing them to “interact” with a world globe, which will then give them all the required information whilst booking their holiday.

Motivation

Of the world’s leading cruise lines, including business’ such as Carnival Cruise Line, Holland America, Norwegian Cruise Line, Royal Caribbean, the vast majority of them offer an extremely basic an objectively boring booking experience, a simple search engine which provides a return list of available cruises which can often be hard to grasp for many potential customers (See example links for common cruise searches). In addition to this, varying studies have shown that in general, a more interactive online experience will increase user satisfaction.
Whilst these list styles will appeal to some visual learners, what it fails to do is capture the “experience” factor and enhance the excitement of travelling to a destination that’s often half the world away. What I hope to do with TouchGlobe (my current name for the project) is provide a booking experience that appeals to not just visual learners, but also kinaesthetic and auditory learners too. Whilst maintaining an efficient and streamlined qualifying/booking experience to maximise revenue.
